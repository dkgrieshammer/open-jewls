Open Jewls

Open Jewls is an open source product design software prototype created for my final thesis of my design studies at KISD. Cobbled together with Processing / Java and the great Hemesh & ControlP5 libraries.

It's pretty alpha and there is great chance to crash the app especially with wiring vronoi-cuts, but you can already export valid .stl-files for printing;
